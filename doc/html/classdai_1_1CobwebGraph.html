◆ DAI_ENUM()

dai::CobwebGraph::DAI_ENUM ( NeighborType  ,
ALL  ,
TOP  ,
CLOSEST   
)

Indicates what happens if a subset of variables in the boundary of a region (\ominus r_p) is shared by some neighbors such that one (\ominus r_{p,q1}) is a subset of another (\ominus r_{p,q2}).

  • ALL all such neighbors are included in the the updates.
  • TOP only (\ominus r_{p,q2}) is included unless (\ominus r_{p,q2} = \ominus r_{p,q1}) in which case both are included
  • CLOSEST (default): similar to TOP but in case of a tie the the region r_q with largest r_q \cap r_p is considered
    Note
    Not important in perfomance!
