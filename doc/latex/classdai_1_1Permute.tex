\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Tool for calculating permutations of linear indices of multi-\/dimensional arrays. 

\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
This is mainly useful for converting indices into multi-\/dimensional arrays corresponding to joint states of variables to and from the canonical ordering used in lib\+DAI. 
\end{DoxyNote}
