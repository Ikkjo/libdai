Enumeration of possible choices for the tree. 

The two possibilities are\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\ttfamily ORG\+:} take the maximum spanning tree where the weights are crude estimates of the mutual information between the nodes;
\item {\ttfamily ALT\+:} take the maximum spanning tree where the weights are upper bounds on the effective interaction strengths between pairs of nodes. 
\end{DoxyItemize}
